Planner settings. The board has 12 tracks split into four 3-track character bands. Each character band owns its own weapon/ammo/reload state. Tap a placed piece to rotate it clockwise. Drag to move. The timeline loops at the last occupied piece column. S/J cells fire the gun and show ▶ icons. Z cells are Knife cells with ⚔ icons, need one open vertical clearance gap, and always slash at the end of the piece. O/L cells reload and show ↻ icons. I/T cells are modifiers and show ✦ icons. A modifier only makes big purple bullets when its time band is complete, that completed band includes the modifier, and the modifier touches a fire piece in that same band. Purple bullets pierce through enemies, Z Knife pieces trigger the Wizard knife special; the knife is separate from gun ammo and fires when valid, and the Enemies setting changes the target simultaneous swarm size up to about 50 enemies on screen. Completed time bands/columns only get a bright outline when the completed band includes at least one modifier cell. Fire overrides reload: reload cells crossed by fire get X'd out. Remaining consecutive reload ticks form red incomplete or green complete reload bands. Use the gear button for these settings and debug tools.
